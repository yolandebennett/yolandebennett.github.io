Nelson Family

Portrait of Phillip Milburn Nelson, c.1943

Phillip Milburn Nelson was born in Jamestown, New York on August 31, 1911, to parents Frederick Allen Nelson and Fanny Belle Carlson Nelson.

He attended Cornell University in Ithaca, New York, and went on to serve in the United States Army during World War II.

Portrait of Fanny Belle Carlson Nelson, c.1890

His mother, Fanny Belle (who preferred to go by Belle), was born in Mayville, New York. She attended the Boston Conservatory of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, which was uncommon for women of her time.
