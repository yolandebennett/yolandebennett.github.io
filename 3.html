Phillip Milburn Nelson

Phillip Milburn Nelson in uniform standing outside, c.1943

Phillip served in the war from April 1943 to December 1945. During that time, he was stationed overseas in England and France.

Throughout the span of his career, Phillip also worked for the Internal Revenue Service, and operated his own accounting firm.

Marietta Duvall Nelson in Chautauqua, New York, c.1950

He met his wife, Marietta, through their work at the same accounting firm in Buffalo, New York.

Marietta later went on to work for Phillip at his firm.
