White Family

Marietta Duvalle Nelson with Phillip Milburn Nelson, Marsha White, and David White, 1942

Marietta had a brother, David White, who was married to Marsha White, and a sister, Elizabeth (Betty) White Riddell, married to Robert Riddell.

Elizabeth White Riddell and husband Robert Riddell at Stromboli Courts, Kissimmee, Florida, 1950

Elizabeth White Riddell with her husband, Robert Riddell, 1954

Betty worked as an English teacher at a Catholic school. She was described as a very sophisticated woman.

She married her husband, Robert Riddell (an engineer), in 1950. Robert died suddenly from a brain aneurysm when he was in his thirties. Betty was never able to fully move on from the loss of her husband.
