Marriage of Phillip & Marietta

Marietta Duvall Nelson with her father, David White, 1938

Phillip had been divorced from a prior marriage by the time he met and began his relationship with Marietta in 1942. Due to their religious beliefs, neither Marietta’s father, nor her aunt Etta (a Catholic nun), initially approved of her wish to marry Phillip. 

As a result, Marietta and Phillip decided to end their relationship. 

Phillip Milburn Nelson with parents Fanny Belle Nelson and Frederick Allen Nelson, 1942

Shortly after his father passed away, Phillip returned home to New York on leave from the war he was serving in. During that time, he contacted Marietta, and they eloped on April 24, 1943.
