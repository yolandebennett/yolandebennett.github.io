Children of Phillip & Marietta

Phillip Milburn Nelson with children Robert, David, and Mary, c.1960

Marietta and Phillip had four children together.

Their first child, Rick, was born in 1946. Their second son, David, was born in 1949, followed by Robert in 1951. Their daughter, Mary, was born in 1957.

Nelson children at family gathering, c.1960

In the photo on the right, children Rick, Robert, and Mary stand with two of their cousins (both wearing striped shirts) at a family reunion in Mayville, New York, moments after Mary fell into a fish pond!
